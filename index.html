Human Resources Information System

An HRIS, short for Human Resources Information System, serves as a comprehensive solution for collecting and storing data related to an organization's workforce. Typically, an HRIS incorporates essential functionalities essential for complete Human Resources Management (HRM), covering areas such as recruitment, performance management, learning and development, leaves, overtimes, payroll and more.

Learning Management System

A learning management system, commonly referred to as LMS, is a software application or web-based technology designed to organize, execute, and evaluate a particular learning procedure. Primarily utilized for e-learning activities, it typically comprises two essential components: a server handling core functions and a user interface (UI) accessible to instructors, students, and administrators.
